Crop Disease Predictor

This Application provides Prediction of Crops Diseases by using Machine Learning Model. Currently it is limited to Cotton Plant.

Plant diseases have turned into a dilemma as it can cause significant reduction in both quality and quantity of agricultural products. Automatic detection of plant diseases is an essential research topic as it may prove benefits in monitoring large fields of crops, and thus automatically detect the symptoms of diseases as soon as they appear on plant leaves.
